# Facts Distiller

Use this skill when turning search results, news coverage, or mixed source notes into a facts-first digest.

## Rules

- Separate facts, attributed claims, interpretation, and open questions.
- A fact needs at least one source URL and should keep the original publication date when available.
- If sources disagree, mark the item `disputed` and name what is disputed.
- If an item is only reported by one source, mark it `reported`, not `confirmed`.
- Do not infer motive, market impact, or policy outcome unless the source evidence directly supports it.
- Keep opinions and recommendations out of the facts list.

## Output Priorities

- What happened.
- Who said or did it.
- When it happened or was reported.
- Which sources support it.
- What remains unverified.
- Which industries or assets could be mechanically affected, with uncertainty preserved.
